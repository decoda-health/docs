<Info>
  **Prerequisites:** Gift Cards must be enabled for your clinic. If the Gift Cards page does not appear under **Settings**, contact your account administrator to turn the module on.
</Info>

Gift cards let patients purchase prepaid value that someone else (or they themselves) can redeem at your clinic later. Each gift card has a unique code, an initial value, and a remaining balance that decreases as it is used.

<Note>
  **Price equals redeemable amount.** Whatever amount the purchaser pays is exactly what the recipient can redeem — there's no surcharge or upcharge. A $100 gift card costs the buyer $100 and gives the recipient $100 of redeemable value.
</Note>

## How Gift Cards Work

There are two ways to put a gift card in a patient's hands:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Issue a one-off gift card" icon="gift" href="#issue-a-one-off-gift-card">
    Quickly create a gift card for any amount, charge the purchaser, and send the code to a recipient — all from a single window.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sell a gift card at checkout" icon="cart-shopping" href="#sell-a-gift-card-at-checkout">
    Add a preset gift card item to a cart during a regular checkout. Useful for fixed denominations like "$50 Gift Card".
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Both methods produce the same result: a unique gift card code with a balance the recipient can spend at your clinic.

## The Gift Cards Settings Page

Go to **Settings** → **Gift Cards**. The page has two sections.

### Gift Card Items

The top section lists the **gift card items** (preset denominations) you sell at checkout — for example, "$50 Gift Card" or "$100 Gift Card". Each item shows:

- **Name** — what staff see when adding it to a cart.
- **Price** — the price the patient pays, which is the same amount the recipient can redeem.
- **Settings** — a **Custom Pricing** badge appears if staff are allowed to override the amount at checkout.

Use the search and filter bar to narrow the list. Click an item to edit it, or use the actions menu to archive it.

### Issued Gift Cards

The bottom section lists every gift card that has been sold or issued, including:

- **Code** — the gift card code (partially masked).
- **Initial Value** — the original amount loaded onto the card.
- **Balance** — how much is left to spend.
- **Status** — see [Gift Card Statuses](#gift-card-statuses) below.
- **Purchaser** — the patient who bought the card.
- **Date Issued** — when the card was created.

Filter by purchaser, recipient, date range, or status. Click a row to open the card's details.

### Gift Card Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | The card has been created but not yet activated. Cards stay pending until the purchase is paid in full. |
| **Active** | The card is fully redeemable. |
| **Disabled** | The card can no longer be redeemed — drained to zero, voided by a purchase refund, or turned off by staff. |

## Create a Gift Card Item

Use a gift card item when you want to offer a fixed denomination — for example, a "$100 Gift Card" SKU that staff can quickly add to a checkout.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Gift Cards page">
    Go to **Settings** → **Gift Cards**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a gift card">
    Click **Add Gift Card** in the **Gift Card Items** section. A side panel opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    - **Name** (required): What staff and patients see — for example, `$50 Gift Card`.
    - **Price** (required): The amount the patient pays. This is the same amount the recipient can redeem.
    - **Description**: Optional details shown to staff.
    - **Allow custom pricing**: Turn this on to let staff override the price when adding the item to a cart. Useful if you want a single "Gift Card" item for any amount.
    - **Allow commission on this gift card**: When on, sales of this gift card item count toward staff commission payouts. Turn it off if you don't want gift card sales to generate commission.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Add Gift Card**. The item is immediately available at checkout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If you only want a single "any amount" gift card item, create one with a starting price (e.g. $50) and turn on **Allow custom pricing**. Staff can then change the amount on the cart line.
</Tip>

## Issue a One-Off Gift Card

Use this when you want to sell a gift card for any amount without going through a full checkout — for example, a walk-in who just wants a gift for a friend.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Gift Cards page">
    Go to **Settings** → **Gift Cards**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start issuing">
    In the **Issued Gift Cards** section, click **Issue gift card**. A window opens.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the gift card details">
    - **Amount** (required): The amount the purchasing patient pays, which is the same amount the recipient can redeem.
    - **Purchasing patient** (required): Search for and select the patient buying the gift card.
    - **Location**: Pick the location the sale belongs to (only shown if your clinic has multiple locations).
    - **Recipient name, email, phone, note**: Optional. Fill these in if the gift card is for someone other than the purchaser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to payment">
    Click **Continue to payment**. The window switches to a payment screen for the purchasing patient. Run the payment as you normally would.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Done">
    Once payment succeeds, the gift card is activated and available to redeem. If you entered a recipient email or phone, the recipient can be notified with the code.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Sell a Gift Card at Checkout

If you've created gift card items as denominations (see [Create a Gift Card Item](#create-a-gift-card-item)), staff can add them to a regular checkout the same way they add any other item.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add the gift card to the cart">
    Open the checkout, search for the gift card item, and add it. If the item allows custom pricing, an edit icon appears next to the price — click it to set the amount the patient is paying. The amount entered is the redeemable balance on the card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a recipient (optional)">
    Each gift card line item shows an **Add recipient** button. Click it to enter the recipient's name, email, phone, and an optional note. If you sold multiple gift cards in the same cart, you can step through each one (e.g., "Recipient 1 of 3") and assign different recipients. Leave the recipient blank to send the card to the purchasing patient.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take payment">
    Complete the checkout as usual. Once paid, each gift card is activated.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Redeem a Gift Card

A gift card can be used as a payment method on any checkout.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get to the payment step">
    Build the cart and continue to the payment step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Gift Card">
    Select **Gift Card** as the payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the code and check balance">
    Type the gift card code (in the format `GC-XXXX-XXXX`) and click **Check Balance**. The remaining balance appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the payment">
    The gift card balance is applied toward the amount due.

    - If the balance covers the full amount, only what's needed is deducted and the rest stays on the card.
    - If the balance is less than the amount due, the **Split Payment** section opens automatically and the gift card is set to cover its full balance. Pick a second payment method (card, cash, check, etc.) to collect the remaining amount in the same checkout.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Gift cards cannot be redeemed until they are **Active**. A gift card stays **Pending** until the purchase that created it has been paid in full.
</Warning>

## Look Up a Gift Card Balance

Recipients can check their own balance without signing in.

- The public balance lookup is available at `/gift-cards/balance` on your clinic's URL.
- The recipient enters the gift card code and sees the initial value and current balance.
- Every gift card notification email and text includes a link to this page, so recipients and purchasers can check the balance later without needing the URL.

Staff can also view balances by opening any issued gift card from the **Issued Gift Cards** list on the settings page.

## Manage an Issued Gift Card

Click a row in the **Issued Gift Cards** section to open its details. From there you can:

- See the gift card code, initial value, current balance, and status. The code is masked by default (e.g., `GC-****-1234`) — click the eye icon to reveal it, and click again to hide.
- See the purchasing patient and the recipient details.
- Resend the gift card to the recipient. The **Send to Recipient** button stays visible even when no recipient contact is on file — it's disabled with a tooltip prompting you to add an email or phone first.
- Send the gift card to the purchaser. A **Send** button next to the purchaser's name lets you deliver the code to the patient who bought it — useful when the card has no recipient on file, or when staff want to send the buyer a copy.
- Activate a **Pending** card manually. An **Activate** button appears next to the status when the card is pending. You can also send a notification to the recipient at the same time.
- See the transaction history of redemptions, refunds, balance adjustments, and the related payment for each.

Both send actions ask for confirmation first and show the contact details that will be used, so you can double-check the email or phone before the notification goes out.

## Advanced

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Custom pricing on gift card items">
    When **Allow custom pricing** is on for a gift card item, staff see an edit icon on the cart line at checkout. Clicking it opens a small window where staff can enter any amount. The amount entered is what the patient pays and what the recipient can redeem — they're always the same number.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Refunds and disabling cards">
    Two kinds of refunds can involve a gift card:

    - **Refunding a charge that was paid with a gift card.** The refund is automatically returned to the original card's balance — there's no other payment method to send it back to. If the card had been drained to zero, it becomes redeemable again.
    - **Refunding the purchase that created a gift card.** The card is voided and can no longer be redeemed. Any future refund of a charge paid with that card must go to another payment method.

    A gift card drained to zero with no outstanding refunds also moves to **Disabled**.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Permissions">
    - **View Inventory** -- Required to view the Gift Cards settings page and the issued gift card list (with the Gift Cards module enabled).
    - **Manage Inventory** -- Required to create or edit gift card items.
    - **Process Payments** -- Required to issue a one-off gift card and take payment for it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
