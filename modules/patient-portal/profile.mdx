---
title: "Profile"
description: "View and update personal information on file with the clinic."
---

**Where to find it:** Click **Profile** in the sidebar.

The profile screen shows the patient's current information on file:

- Name and preferred name
- Date of birth and gender
- Contact details (email, phone)
- Address

## Update your information

1. Click **Edit Profile**.
2. Update any fields.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

Changes are saved immediately and reflected in the clinic's records.
