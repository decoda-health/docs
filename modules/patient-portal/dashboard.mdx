When a patient signs in, the dashboard is the first screen they see.

## Calendar

The calendar shows the current month. Days that have appointments display a dot underneath the date number. Clicking a date filters the appointment list below to show only appointments on that day. Clicking the same date again clears the filter.

Use the **<** and **>** arrows to move between months.

## Upcoming Appointments

The card on the right shows the next few upcoming appointments. Each entry shows the service name, date and time, provider, and location. Clicking an appointment opens its detail page.

## Pending Forms

Below the appointments card, any forms the patient has not yet completed are listed. Each form shows whether it has been started or not yet started. Clicking a form opens it in a new tab.

A green checkmark appears when there are no pending forms.

## Quick Actions

The Quick Actions card provides shortcuts to the most common tasks:

- **Book Appointment** — opens the self-scheduling page in a new tab.
- **Complete a Form** — opens the forms list.
- **Update Profile** — opens the profile edit screen.

### Customize quick actions

1. Click **Edit** in the Quick Actions card.
2. Click the **X** next to any action to remove it.
3. Click **+** next to any action to add it back.
4. Click **Done** when finished.

The layout is saved for the next time the patient signs in.
