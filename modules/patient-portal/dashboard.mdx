title: "Dashboard"
description: "The patient portal home screen — upcoming appointments, pending forms, recent visits, and quick actions."
---

When a patient signs in, the dashboard is the first screen they see. It shows a personalized greeting (e.g., "Good morning, Sarah") with today's date, followed by a grid of cards summarizing the patient's appointments, forms, and quick actions.

## Upcoming Appointments

The largest card on the dashboard shows up to 5 upcoming appointments. Each entry displays the service name, date and time, provider, and location. Clicking an appointment opens its detail page.

If the patient has no upcoming appointments, a **Book Appointment Now** button appears in this card.

## Pending Forms

This card shows up to 3 forms the patient has not yet completed. Each form shows whether it is **In progress** or **Not started**. Clicking a form opens it so the patient can continue or begin filling it out.

## Completed Forms

This card shows up to 5 forms the patient has already submitted, along with the completion date. Clicking a completed form opens a read-only view of their responses.

## Recent Visits

This card shows up to 5 past appointments with a date badge showing the month and day. This gives patients a quick view of their recent visit history.

## Quick Actions

The Quick Actions card provides shortcuts to the most common tasks:

- **Book Appointment** -- opens the self-scheduling page in a new tab.
- **Complete a Form** -- opens the forms list.
- **View Appointments** -- opens the appointments page.
- **Update Profile** -- opens the profile edit screen.

### Customize Quick Actions

Click **Edit** in the Quick Actions card to toggle into edit mode. Remove an action by clicking the **X** on it, or add an available action by clicking the **+** button. Click **Done** when finished.

The layout is saved for the next time the patient signs in.
