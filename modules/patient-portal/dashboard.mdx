---

When a patient signs in, the dashboard is the first screen they see. It opens with a personalized greeting (e.g., "Good morning, Sarah") followed by a grid of widgets the clinic has chosen for that screen.

## Available widgets

You decide which of these appear on the patient's dashboard, in what order, and how wide each one is.

| Widget | What it shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Calendar** | A month calendar that highlights appointment days. Selecting a day shows that day's visits. If the patient has no upcoming visits, a **Book Appointment** button takes its place. |
| **Upcoming Appointments** | A short list of the next few visits with date, time, provider, and location. |
| **Next Appointment** | A highlighted banner for the very next visit, including a countdown. |
| **Forms** | Pending and completed forms, with a tab switch between the two. Patients open a form to fill it out or to view the answers they submitted. |
| **Quick Actions** | Shortcuts to common tasks — book an appointment, complete a form, view appointments, update profile. Patients can edit which actions appear in this widget. |
| **Recent Visits** | A list of past appointments, most recent first. |
| **My Memberships** | Active membership plans with their key benefits. |
| **Billing** | The patient's upcoming billing cycle and recent charges. |
| **Credit Balance** | Account credit available to use against future charges. |
| **Insurance** | Insurance carrier and member ID on file. |
| **Pre-Visit Checklist** | A preparation checklist for the next visit. |
| **Visit Summary** | The patient's visit count and engagement summary for the year. |
| **Measurements** | Recent measurement readings configured to show in the portal. |

## Customize the dashboard

**Where to find it:** Go to **Settings** > **Patient Portal**. The **Layout** tab is open by default.

The Layout tab shows a preview of the patient dashboard. Use it to arrange widgets:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reorder widgets">
    Drag a widget by its header to move it. The grid reflows as you drag.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resize a widget">
    Use the size switch in each widget's header to set its width: **1/3**, **1/2**, **2/3**, or **Full**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hide a widget">
    Click the eye icon in a widget's header. The widget moves into the **Hidden Widgets** section below the preview.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Show a hidden widget">
    Find it in **Hidden Widgets** and click **Add**. It returns to the dashboard at the end of the visible list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** at the top of the page. The new layout applies to every patient the next time they sign in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Click **Reset to defaults** to discard your changes and restore the standard layout.

<Note>
  The same layout is shared by every patient at your clinic. There is no per-patient layout. The exception is the **Quick Actions** widget, where each patient can pick their own shortcuts.
</Note>

## Customize Quick Actions (patient-side)

Inside the Quick Actions widget, each patient can pick which shortcuts they see. The patient clicks **Edit** in the widget, removes an action with the **X** on it, adds an available action with the **+** button, and clicks **Done**. Their choices stick for next time they sign in.
