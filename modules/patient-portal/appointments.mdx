---
title: "Appointments"
description: "View upcoming and past appointments, and cancel if needed."
---

**Where to find it:** Click **Appointments** in the sidebar.

## Upcoming

Upcoming appointments are grouped into three sections:

- **Today** — appointments happening today.
- **This Week** — appointments in the next 7 days, not including today.
- **Coming Up** — all appointments after this week.

Each appointment shows the service name, date and time, provider, and location. Clicking an appointment opens its detail page.

## Past

Past appointments are listed in reverse chronological order, grouped by month. Clicking any past appointment opens its detail page.

## Cancel an appointment

1. Click the appointment to open its detail page.
2. Click **Cancel Appointment**.
3. Optionally enter a reason.
4. Confirm the cancellation.

Cancelled appointments move to the Cancelled tab.
