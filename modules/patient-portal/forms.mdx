title: "Forms"
description: "Complete pending forms and view previously submitted forms."
---

**Where to find it:** Click **Forms** in the sidebar.

## Pending

Lists all forms assigned to the patient that have not been fully submitted. Each form shows whether it is in progress or not yet started.

Click **Open Form** to open the form in a new tab and fill it out.

A green checkmark screen appears when there are no pending forms.

## Completed

Lists all forms the patient has already submitted, with the date each was completed. Clicking a completed form opens a read-only view of the submitted answers.
