---
title: 'Document Types'
description: 'Manage the categories used to classify patient documents across your clinic.'
---

Document types let you organize patient documents into meaningful categories like "Lab", "Image", "Report", or any custom label your clinic needs. When staff upload a document, they select a type from a searchable list. Patients can then filter their documents by type on the patient page.

Your clinic comes with several built-in types: **Lab**, **Pharmacy**, **Image**, **Report**, **Other**, **Consent**, and **Note Upload**. You can add as many custom types as you need.

## Add a Document Type

New document types are created inline when uploading a document:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload a Document">
    On a patient's **Forms & Documents** tab, click **Upload Document**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search or Create a Type">
    In the **Document Type** field, start typing. If the type you need doesn't exist yet, an **Add** option appears at the bottom of the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the New Type">
    Click the **Add** option. The new type is created and selected automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Filtering Documents by Type

On a patient's **Forms & Documents** tab, a type filter appears next to the Documents heading whenever the patient has documents. Select a type from the list to show only documents of that category, or choose **All types** to see everything.
