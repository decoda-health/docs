## Mobile App Installation

Decoda is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA) that you can install on your iOS or Android device. Once installed, you can access Decoda directly from your home screen with a native app-like experience.

## Installation Instructions

### iOS (iPhone & iPad)

To install Decoda on your iOS device:

1. Open Decoda in Safari (the app must be opened in Safari, not other browsers)
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
3. Scroll down in the share menu and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Customize the name if desired, then tap **"Add"** to confirm

The Decoda app icon will now appear on your home screen. Tap it to launch the app in standalone mode.

### Android & Chrome

To install Decoda on your Android device or Chrome browser:

1. Open Decoda in Chrome or another Chromium-based browser
2. Look for the install prompt that appears, or:
   - Tap the **menu** (three dots) in the top-right corner
   - Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
3. Confirm the installation when prompted

The Decoda app will be installed and accessible from your home screen or app drawer.

## Benefits

Once installed, the Decoda mobile app provides:

- **Quick Access**: Launch Decoda directly from your home screen
- **Offline Capability**: Access cached content even without an internet connection
- **Native Experience**: App-like interface optimized for mobile devices
- **Faster Loading**: Improved performance with service worker caching
- **Push Notifications**: Receive important updates and alerts (when enabled)

## Troubleshooting

### iOS Installation Issues

- **Can't find "Add to Home Screen"**: Make sure you're using Safari, not Chrome or other browsers
- **Share button not visible**: Try scrolling the page or refreshing
- **Installation fails**: Ensure you have sufficient storage space and try again

### Android Installation Issues

- **Install prompt doesn't appear**: Check your browser settings to ensure PWA installation is enabled
- **App won't install**: Clear your browser cache and try again
- **Icon missing**: The app may be in your app drawer rather than your home screen

## Updating the App

The Decoda mobile app updates automatically when you have an internet connection. You'll be prompted to refresh when a new version is available. Simply reload the app to get the latest features and improvements.
