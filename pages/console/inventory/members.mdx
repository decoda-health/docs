# Membership Members

View and manage patients enrolled in specific memberships. Track usage, credits, and manage enrollment status.

## View Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access from Memberships">
    Click member count on a membership row
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse member list">
    See patient name, enrollment date, status, and credits
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search members">
    Find specific patients
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Member Details

Each member entry shows:
- Patient name and contact
- Enrollment date
- Billing status
- Remaining credits
- Next billing date

## Manage Enrollment

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pause Membership" icon="pause">
    Temporarily suspend billing
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cancel Membership" icon="xmark">
    End the patient's membership
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## View Credit Usage

Click on a member to see:
- Total credits allocated
- Credits used
- Remaining balance
- Usage history

<Tip>
Credits may roll over or expire based on membership settings.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Member not found | Check they're enrolled in this membership |
| Credits incorrect | Review usage history |
| Can't cancel | Check permissions |
