# Memberships

Memberships let you offer patients recurring plans with ongoing benefits - like monthly discounts, included services, or credits they can use toward treatments.

## Viewing Your Memberships

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Memberships">
    Click **Inventory** in the menu, then **Memberships**
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your plans">
    Each row shows a membership with its price, how many members you have, and total revenue
  </Step>
  <Step title="View details">
    Click the arrow on the left to expand and see what's included
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What's Included in a Membership?

A membership can include any combination of:

- **Credits** - A monthly dollar amount patients can use toward any service
- **Discounts** - Percentage off certain services or products
- **Included services** - Specific treatments covered by the membership
- **Rollover** - Whether unused credits carry over to next month

## Creating a New Membership

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add Membership">
    Top right corner of the page
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the basics">
    - **Name** - What patients will see (e.g., "Gold Membership")
    - **Price** - How much to charge
    - **Billing frequency** - Monthly, quarterly, or annually
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add benefits">
    Choose what members get:
    - Monthly credits to spend
    - Discounts on services
    - Free included services
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your membership is ready for enrollment
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Enrolling a Patient

There are two ways to enroll patients:

### From the Membership Page
<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the membership">
    Locate it in your list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the menu">
    The three dots (⋮) on the right
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Add New Member">
    Search for and select the patient
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete enrollment">
    Set the start date and payment method
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From the Patient's Profile
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the patient">
    Open their profile
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Memberships tab">
    Click the Memberships tab on the left
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Enroll">
    Choose which membership to add them to
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Members

To see who's enrolled in a membership:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the membership">
    Locate it in your list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the member count">
    The number in the "Members" column is a link
  </Step>
  <Step title="View member list">
    See all enrolled patients with their status and next billing date
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing a Membership

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the menu">
    Three dots (⋮) on the membership row
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Edit">
    Opens the membership settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make changes">
    Update name, price, or benefits
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Changes apply to new enrollments (existing members keep their current terms)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Changing a membership's price or benefits affects new members only. Existing members keep their original terms unless you update them individually.
</Warning>

## Archiving a Membership

No longer offering a membership? Archive it:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the menu">
    Three dots (⋮) on the row
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Archive">
    Confirm the action
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Archiving prevents new enrollments, but existing members keep their membership and benefits until they cancel.
</Info>

## Billing Frequencies

| Option | When patients are charged |
|--------|---------------------------|
| Monthly | Every month |
| Quarterly | Every 3 months |
| Semi-annually | Every 6 months |
| Annually | Once a year |

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens if a member's payment fails?">
    You'll receive an alert, and the patient will be notified to update their payment method. Their benefits may be suspended until payment is successful.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can members cancel anytime?">
    Yes, you can cancel a membership at any time from the patient's profile. You can also configure whether to prorate their final month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do unused credits roll over?">
    This depends on how you set up the membership. You can configure whether credits expire at the end of each month or roll over.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I have different membership tiers?">
    Yes! Create multiple memberships with different price points and benefits (e.g., Silver, Gold, Platinum).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't archive membership | Make sure there are no active members, or archive anyway and existing members will remain |
| Member count looks wrong | Try refreshing the page |
| Benefits not applying at checkout | Verify the membership is active and the patient is enrolled |
| Can't see member details | Click the member count to view the list |
