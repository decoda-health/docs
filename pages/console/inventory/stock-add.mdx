# Add Stock

Record stock additions when receiving new inventory. Track quantities, costs, and supplier information.

## Add Stock

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select product">
    Choose the product to restock
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter quantity">
    Amount being added
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter cost (optional)">
    Unit cost for this batch
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add notes">
    Reference number, supplier, or other notes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Stock level increases
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Batch Addition

Add stock for multiple products at once:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Batch Add">
    Opens multi-product form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add products">
    Select each product and quantity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review totals">
    Verify all items
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save all">
    All stock levels update
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use batch addition when receiving a shipment with multiple products.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Product not found | Check product is set to track inventory |
| Can't save | Verify quantity is valid number |
