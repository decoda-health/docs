# Stock Management

Track and manage inventory stock levels. View current stock, make adjustments, and review stock history.

  </Step>

## Adjust Stock

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find product">
    Locate the product in the list
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Adjust">
    Opens adjustment form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter adjustment">
    - Set new quantity, or
    - Enter +/- adjustment amount
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add reason">
    Select reason: restock, sale, damage, count adjustment
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Stock level updates
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Stock Status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **In Stock** | Above reorder threshold |
| **Low Stock** | Below threshold but not zero |
| **Out of Stock** | Zero quantity |

<Warning>
Low stock items may trigger alerts if notifications are configured.
</Warning>

## View History

Click on a product to see its stock adjustment history:
- Date and time
- Previous quantity
- New quantity
- Reason
- User who made the change

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Stock not updating | Refresh page after adjustment |
| Can't adjust | Check permissions |
| History missing | Check date range filter |
