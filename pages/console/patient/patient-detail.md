# Patient Detail Page - User Actions Guide

## Overview

The Patient Detail page is the comprehensive patient profile view, containing all information about a patient organized into tabs. It includes overview, timeline, appointments, medical notes, billing, forms/documents, gallery, memberships, packages, and settings.

---

## Page Layout

### Left Sidebar
- Patient header (name, photo, contact)
- Duplicate warning banner (if applicable)
- Tab navigation with counts

### Main Content Area
- Selected tab content
- Full-width content display
- Scrollable area

---

## Available Actions

### 1. Navigate Between Tabs

**Purpose:** Switch between different sections of the patient profile.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Locate the tab navigation**
   - Left sidebar on desktop
   - Drawer menu on mobile

2. **View available tabs:**
   - **Overview** - Summary information
   - **Timeline** - Activity history
   - **Appointments** - Past and upcoming appointments
   - **Medical Notes & Data** - Clinical notes, measurements, medications
   - **Billing** - Charges, payments, payment methods
   - **Forms & Documents** - Submissions and uploads
   - **Gallery** - Photos and attachments
   - **Memberships** - Active memberships
   - **Packages** - Purchased packages
   - **Settings** - Patient preferences

3. **Click on a tab**
   - Tab becomes highlighted
   - Content area updates
   - URL updates with tab parameter

4. **View tab counts**
   - Numbers next to tab names
   - Shows quantity of items in each section

---

### 2. View Patient Overview Tab

**Purpose:** See summary of patient information at a glance.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Overview tab**
   - Click "Overview" in sidebar
   - Or open patient page (default tab)

2. **View patient information:**
   - **Contact Info:** Phone, email, address
   - **Demographics:** DOB, age, gender, pronouns
   - **Recent Appointments:** Upcoming and past visits
   - **Quick Notes:** Staff notes about patient
   - **Recent Charges:** Outstanding balances
   - **Form Status:** Pending forms

3. **Edit patient info:**
   - Click edit icon next to sections
   - Modal opens for editing
   - Save changes

4. **Add quick notes:**
   - Click "Add Note" or note input
   - Type your note
   - Save - appears in patient record

---

### 3. View Patient Timeline Tab

**Purpose:** See chronological history of all patient activity.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Timeline tab**
   - Click "Timeline" in sidebar

2. **View activity feed:**
   - Events listed chronologically
   - Most recent at top
   - Infinite scroll for history

3. **Activity types shown:**
   - Appointments (scheduled, completed, cancelled)
   - Payments made
   - Forms submitted
   - Notes added
   - Messages sent/received
   - Status changes

4. **Click activity for details:**
   - Expand activity item
   - View full details
   - Navigate to related item

---

### 4. Manage Appointments Tab

**Purpose:** View and manage patient's appointments.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Appointments tab**
   - Click "Appointments" in sidebar
   - Count shows total appointments

2. **View appointment sections:**
   - **Upcoming:** Future scheduled appointments
   - **Past:** Historical appointments
   - **Cancelled:** Cancelled appointments

3. **View appointment details:**
   - Click on an appointment
   - See date, time, provider, service
   - View status and notes

4. **Schedule new appointment:**
   - Click "New Appointment" button
   - Opens scheduling interface
   - Patient pre-selected

5. **Reschedule appointment:**
   - Click appointment, then "Reschedule"
   - Select new date/time
   - Confirm change

6. **Cancel appointment:**
   - Click appointment, then "Cancel"
   - Select cancellation reason
   - Confirm cancellation

---

### 5. Manage Medical Notes & Data Tab

**Purpose:** Access clinical documentation, measurements, and medications.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Medical Notes & Data tab**
   - Click "Medical Notes & Data" in sidebar

2. **View sections:**

   **Notes:**
   - Clinical notes from providers
   - Scribe-generated notes
   - Click to view full note

   **Measurements:**
   - Vitals, weight, height, etc.
   - Add new measurement
   - View historical data

   **Medications:**
   - Current medications
   - Prescribed by practice
   - Self-reported medications

3. **Add a new note:**
   - Click "Add Note"
   - Select note template
   - Fill in note content
   - Save note

4. **Add measurement:**
   - Click "Add Measurement"
   - Select measurement type
   - Enter value
   - Save

5. **Add medication:**
   - Click "Add Medication"
   - Search medication database
   - Enter dosage/frequency
   - Save

---

### 6. Manage Billing Tab

**Purpose:** View and manage patient's financial transactions.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Billing tab**
   - Click "Billing" in sidebar
   - Count shows charge count

2. **View sections:**

   **Charges:**
   - Outstanding charges
   - Paid charges
   - Click for details

   **Payments:**
   - Payment history
   - View receipts

   **Payment Methods:**
   - Saved cards/accounts
   - Add new method
   - Set default

   **Banked Items:**
   - Credit balances
   - Prepaid services

3. **Create new charge:**
   - Click "New Charge"
   - Add items/services
   - Process or save

4. **Process payment:**
   - Select charge(s)
   - Click "Process Payment"
   - Enter payment details
   - Complete transaction

5. **Add payment method:**
   - Click "Add Payment Method"
   - Enter card details
   - Save for future use

6. **Issue refund:**
   - Select payment
   - Click "Refund"
   - Enter amount
   - Confirm refund

---

### 7. Manage Forms & Documents Tab

**Purpose:** View form submissions and uploaded documents.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Forms & Documents tab**
   - Click "Forms & Documents" in sidebar

2. **View sections:**

   **Form Submissions:**
   - Completed forms
   - Pending forms
   - Click to view submission

   **Documents:**
   - Uploaded files
   - PDFs, images, etc.

3. **View form submission:**
   - Click on submission
   - See all responses
   - View signature if present
   - Download PDF

4. **Send form to patient:**
   - Click "Send Form"
   - Select form type
   - Choose delivery method (SMS/email)
   - Send

5. **Upload document:**
   - Click "Upload Document"
   - Select file from computer
   - Add description
   - Upload

6. **Download document:**
   - Click on document
   - Click download button
   - File downloads

---

### 8. View Gallery Tab

**Purpose:** View photos and media attached to patient.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Gallery tab**
   - Click "Gallery" in sidebar
   - Count shows attachment count

2. **Browse images:**
   - Thumbnails displayed in grid
   - Click to view full size

3. **Upload new image:**
   - Click "Upload" or drag-and-drop
   - Select image file(s)
   - Add description/tags
   - Upload

4. **View image details:**
   - Click on image
   - Full-size viewer opens
   - See date uploaded
   - Navigate between images

5. **Delete image:**
   - Select image
   - Click delete button
   - Confirm deletion

---

### 9. Manage Memberships Tab

**Purpose:** View patient's active memberships.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Memberships tab**
   - Click "Memberships" in sidebar

2. **View memberships:**
   - Active memberships listed
   - Membership name and tier
   - Start date
   - Benefits included

3. **Enroll in membership:**
   - Click "Add Membership"
   - Select membership type
   - Set start date
   - Process payment

4. **Cancel membership:**
   - Click on membership
   - Click "Cancel"
   - Select effective date
   - Confirm cancellation

---

### 10. Manage Packages Tab

**Purpose:** View purchased treatment packages and usage.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Packages tab**
   - Click "Packages" in sidebar
   - Count shows package count

2. **View packages:**
   - Active packages
   - Remaining credits/sessions
   - Expiration dates

3. **View package usage:**
   - Click on package
   - See usage history
   - Remaining balance

4. **Purchase new package:**
   - Click "Add Package"
   - Select package type
   - Process payment
   - Package added to patient

---

### 11. Manage Settings Tab

**Purpose:** Configure patient-specific preferences and access.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Settings tab**
   - Click "Settings" in sidebar

2. **Configure settings:**

   **Communication Preferences:**
   - SMS opt-in/out for appointment reminders
   - Marketing message preferences

   **Patient Portal:**
   - View portal access status
   - Send portal invitation
   - Reset portal password

   **Account Management:**
   - Archive patient record
   - Delete patient (if permitted)

3. **Changes apply immediately**
   - Toggle switches save automatically
   - No manual save button required

---

### 12. Handle Duplicate Patient Warning

**Purpose:** Resolve potential duplicate patient records.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Notice the warning banner**
   - Yellow/amber banner below patient header
   - Text: "Possible Duplicates Detected! Resolve →"

2. **Click the warning banner**
   - Navigates to patient merge page
   - Current patient and duplicates pre-selected

3. **Complete merge process**
   - Review patient data
   - Select primary record
   - Merge duplicates
   - See Merge page documentation

---

### 13. Access Mobile Navigation

**Purpose:** Navigate tabs on mobile devices.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Locate the menu button**
   - Hamburger icon in patient header
   - Visible on mobile devices

2. **Tap to open drawer**
   - Left-side drawer slides in
   - Tab navigation displayed

3. **Select a tab**
   - Tap on tab name
   - Drawer closes
   - Content updates

4. **Close drawer**
   - Tap outside drawer
   - Or tap X button

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Patient not found | Check ID in URL, verify access |
| Tab not loading | Refresh page, check permissions |
| Duplicate warning missing | Patient already merged or no duplicates |
| Can't edit field | Check permissions, field may be locked |
| Attachments not uploading | Check file size/type limits |
