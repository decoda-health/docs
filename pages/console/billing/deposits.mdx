# Deposits

The Deposits page displays patient credit balances and deposit transactions. View deposit history, apply credits to charges, and manage refunds.

## View Deposits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Deposits">
    Go to **Billing** → **Payments** → Click **View Deposits**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review deposit list">
    See patient, amount, date, and status for each deposit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter">
    Use date range and patient filters
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Deposits

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Patient Credits" icon="coins">
    Pre-paid balances that can be applied to future charges

  <Card title="Refundable Deposits" icon="hand-holding-dollar">
    Deposits that can be returned to the patient
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Apply Deposit to Charge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to checkout">
    Start a new charge for the patient
  </Step>
  <Step title="View available credit">
    Patient's credit balance shown
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply credit">
    Select to apply toward the charge
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Refund Deposit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select deposit">
    Find the deposit to refund
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open actions menu">
    Click the three dots (⋮)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Refund">
    Process refund to original payment method
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Deposit not showing | Check date filters |
| Can't apply credit | Verify patient has available balance |
| Refund failed | Check original payment method is valid |
