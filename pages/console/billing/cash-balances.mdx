# Cash Register

The Cash Balances page tracks cash register balances, allowing staff to view current drawer balance, add/remove cash, and reconcile discrepancies.

## Adjust Cash Balance

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Add/Remove Cash">
    Opens adjustment modal
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter amount">
    Positive to add, negative to remove
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add reason">
    Note why adjustment was made
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Balance updates
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reconcile Discrepancies

<Warning>
Discrepancies between expected and actual cash should be investigated and documented.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Count drawer">
    Physically count cash in register
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter actual amount">
    Record the counted amount
  </Step>
  <Step title="Acknowledge discrepancy">
    If amounts don't match, document the difference
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Close Register

Click **Close Register** at end of day to complete the closing procedure. See the Close Register page for detailed steps.

| Issue | Solution |
