# Payments

The Payments page displays all payment transactions with filtering, sorting, and management capabilities. View payment details, process refunds, send receipts, move payments between patients, and create one-time payments.

## View Payments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payments">
    Go to **Billing** → **Payments**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review payments table">
    Each row shows amount (in green), patient, payment method, medium, and date
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter payments">
    Use date range, status, payment medium, patient, or merchant account filters
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create One-Time Payment

Record a payment not tied to a specific charge.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click One Time Payment">
    Button in header area
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Select patient, enter amount, choose payment medium
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Payment recorded and appears in table
  </Step>
</Steps>

## View Payment Timeline

Click the expand arrow (▼) on a payment row to see:
- Payment creation details
- Related charge information
- Refund history (if any)

## Payment Actions

### Refund Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open actions menu">
    Click the three dots (⋮) on payment row
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Refund Payment">
    Refund modal opens
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure refund">
    Full or partial amount with reason
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process">
    Funds returned, payment shows "Refunded" badge
  </Step>
</Steps>

### View/Send Receipt

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open actions menu">
    Select **Receipt Details**
  </Step>
  <Step title="View receipt">
    See amount, date, method, and items paid
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send receipt">
    Enter email or phone, click Send
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Move Payment

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Move to Another Patient" icon="user">
    Transfer payment to a different patient. Search and select destination patient.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Move to Location" icon="location-dot">
    Change payment's location assignment. Select new location from list.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Generate Detailed Receipt

For payments with linked charges, select **Generate Detailed Receipt** to open a comprehensive printable receipt in a new tab.

## Payment Status Badges

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **Succeeded** | Payment completed successfully |
| **Processing** | Payment in progress |
| **Refunded** | Payment has been refunded |
| **Refund Failed** | Refund attempt failed |
| **Payment Plan** | Part of scheduled payments |

## Payment Methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Card | Credit/debit card |
| Cash | Cash payment |
| Check | Check payment |
| POS Terminal | In-person terminal |
| Patient Credit | Applied credit balance |
| Insurance | Insurance payment |

## Export Payments

Click **Export Payments** to download filtered payment data as CSV or Excel.

<Tip>
Click **View Deposits** in the header to navigate to deposit/credit transactions.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Payment not showing | Check date range filters |
| Can't refund | Check if already refunded |
| Move failed | Verify permissions |
| Export empty | Adjust filters |
