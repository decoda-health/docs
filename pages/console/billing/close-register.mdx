# Close Register

The Close Register page guides staff through the end-of-day register closing procedure, including counting cash, reconciling totals, and completing the daily close.

## Closing Procedure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Count physical cash">
    Count all bills and coins in the drawer
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter counted amount">
    Input the total cash counted
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review expected amount">
    System shows expected total based on transactions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Acknowledge discrepancy">
    If amounts differ, document the variance
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete close">
    Finalize the register close
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Daily Summary

The closing screen displays:
- Total cash sales for the day
- Cash received
- Cash removed (change, adjustments)
- Expected closing balance
- Actual closing balance
- Variance (if any)

<Info>
Register close records are saved for auditing and reporting purposes.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't close register | Ensure all pending transactions are complete |
| Large discrepancy | Review day's transactions, recount cash |
| Close button disabled | Check permissions |
