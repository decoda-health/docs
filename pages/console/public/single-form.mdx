# Single Form

The single form page displays one specific form for patient completion. Used when a specific form is sent directly to a patient.

## Form Completion

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access via link">
    Patient opens the unique form link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify identity">
    May need to confirm patient info
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete questions">
    Fill in all required fields
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Form saved to patient record
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

- Auto-save progress
- Required field validation
- Conditional logic shows/hides questions
- Mobile-optimized interface
- Electronic signature support
