# Payment Link

Payment links provide a direct way for patients to pay specific amounts. Generate and share links for invoices or custom amounts.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Patient receives link">
    Via SMS, email, or shared directly
  </Step>
  <Step title="Opens payment page">
    Sees amount and practice info
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enters card details">
    Secure payment form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Completes payment">
    Confirmation provided
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Link Features

- Pre-filled amount
- Practice branding
- Expiration date option
- Mobile-friendly
- Secure payment processing
