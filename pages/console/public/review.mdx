# Review

The review page collects patient feedback after appointments. Patients can rate their experience and leave comments.

## Submit Review

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access review link">
    From post-appointment message
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rate experience">
    Star rating or satisfaction scale
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add comments">
    Optional feedback text
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Review saved
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Review Options

Depending on configuration:
- Star rating (1-5)
- NPS score (0-10)
- Category ratings (staff, wait time, etc.)
- Open text comments

## Redirect Options

After submission, patients may be:
- Thanked on-page
- Redirected to Google/Yelp for public review (high ratings)
- Asked for private feedback (lower ratings)
