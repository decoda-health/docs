# Reschedule Appointment

Patients can reschedule their appointments through this self-service page.

<Info>
Accessed via links in appointment reminders or confirmation messages.
</Info>

## Reschedule Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access reschedule link">
    From SMS or email
  </Step>
  <Step title="View current appointment">
    See existing booking details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select new time">
    Browse available slots
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm change">
    Appointment updated
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Restrictions

Rescheduling may be limited by:
- Minimum notice requirement (e.g., 24 hours)
- Maximum reschedules allowed
- Practice-specific policies
