# Self-Schedule Page - User Actions Guide

## Overview

The Self-Schedule page enables patients to book their own appointments online. It provides a step-by-step booking flow with service selection, provider selection, location selection, time selection, and checkout.

---

## Available Actions

### 1. Resume Previous Booking Progress

**Purpose:** Continue a booking session that was previously started but not completed.

**When This Appears:**
- You previously started booking but didn't complete it
- Your booking progress was saved in browser storage
- You return to the self-schedule page

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Wait for the resume prompt dialog**
   - A dialog box appears asking "Welcome back!"
   - The message explains that your previous progress was saved

2. **Choose your option:**

   **To Continue Previous Booking:**
   - Click the "Continue" button (highlighted/primary button)
   - You'll be taken back to where you left off
   - All previous selections will be restored

   **To Start Over:**
   - Click the "Start over" button
   - All previous selections will be cleared
   - You'll begin the booking process from the first step

3. **Proceed with booking**
   - Continue with the appropriate step based on your choice

---

### 2. Select Patient Type (New vs. Returning)

**Purpose:** Indicate whether you are a new or returning patient.

**When This Step Appears:**
- This is typically the first step in the booking flow
- May be skipped if only one option is applicable

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **View the patient type options**
   - "New Patient" - You've never visited this practice before
   - "Returning Patient" - You've visited this practice before

2. **Click your appropriate selection**
   - Cards/buttons for each option are displayed
   - Click on the one that applies to you

3. **Automatic progression**
   - After selection, you'll automatically move to the next step
   - A checkmark animation may appear to confirm your selection

---

### 3. Select Location Type

**Purpose:** Choose whether you want an in-person or virtual appointment.

**When This Step Appears:**
- After patient type selection (if applicable)
- Only shows if the practice offers multiple location types

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **View available location types**
   - "In-Person" - Visit the physical clinic
   - "Virtual/Telehealth" - Online video appointment
   - "Phone" - Telephone consultation (if offered)

2. **Click your preferred option**
   - Each option displays as a clickable card
   - Click on your preferred appointment type

3. **Proceed to next step**
   - The page automatically advances
   - A confirmation animation appears

---

### 4. Select Service(s)

**Purpose:** Choose the service or treatment you want to book.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Browse available services**
   - Services are displayed as cards or a list
   - Each service shows:
     - Service name
     - Duration (e.g., "30 min")
     - Price (if displayed)
     - Brief description (if available)

2. **Filter or search services (if available)**
   - Use the search box to find specific services
   - Click category filters to narrow options

3. **Select your service**
   - Click on the service card you want
   - The selected service is highlighted
   - For practices allowing multiple services:
     - Click additional services to add them
     - Click a selected service again to deselect

4. **Review selection**
   - Selected services appear highlighted or checked
   - Total duration may be displayed

5. **Proceed to next step**
   - Click "Continue" or "Next" button
   - Or the page may auto-advance after selection

**Special Cases:**

- **Single service selection:** Most common - select one service
- **Multiple services:** Some practices allow booking multiple services
- **Package services:** Some services include multiple components

---

### 5. Select Provider

**Purpose:** Choose which provider (doctor, therapist, etc.) you want to see.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **View available providers**
   - Providers are shown as cards with:
     - Photo (if available)
     - Name
     - Title/specialty
     - Brief bio (expandable)

2. **Review provider information**
   - Click on provider cards to see more details
   - Check credentials, specialties, and reviews

3. **Select your preferred provider**
   - Click "Select" on your chosen provider
   - The provider card becomes highlighted

4. **"Any Provider" option (if available)**
   - Click "Any Available Provider" if you have no preference
   - This may show more time slot options

5. **Proceed to next step**
   - Click "Continue" or the page auto-advances

**Note:** If only one provider is available, this step may be skipped automatically.

---

### 6. Select Location

**Purpose:** Choose which physical location to visit (for in-person appointments).

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **View available locations**
   - Locations display as cards with:
     - Location name
     - Full address
     - Phone number
     - Hours of operation
     - Distance from you (if location access granted)

2. **Review location details**
   - Check address and hours
   - Verify parking or transit information if shown

3. **Select your location**
   - Click on the location card
   - It becomes highlighted to show selection

4. **Proceed to time selection**
   - Click "Continue" or auto-advance

---

### 7. Select Date and Time

**Purpose:** Choose when you want your appointment.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **View the calendar**
   - A calendar displays available dates
   - Available dates are highlighted/selectable
   - Unavailable dates are grayed out

2. **Navigate between months**
   - Click the left arrow (<) to go to the previous month
   - Click the right arrow (>) to go to the next month
   - Current month name and year are displayed at top

3. **Select a date**
   - Click on an available date
   - The date becomes highlighted
   - Available time slots for that date appear

4. **Browse time slots**
   - Time slots display in chronological order
   - Each slot shows the start time (e.g., "10:00 AM")
   - Scroll through available times

5. **Select a time slot**
   - Click on your preferred time
   - The slot becomes highlighted
   - Confirm your selection

6. **Review your selection**
   - Selected date and time are displayed prominently
   - Verify it matches your availability

7. **Proceed to confirmation**
   - Click "Continue" or "Book This Time"

**Time Slot Considerations:**

- Available slots depend on provider availability
- Some slots may become unavailable during your selection (booked by others)
- Different time zones may be indicated

---

### 8. View and Edit Booking Confirmation

**Purpose:** Review all booking details before finalizing.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Review the confirmation summary**
   - Service(s) selected
   - Provider name
   - Location address
   - Date and time
   - Estimated duration
   - Price (if applicable)

2. **Edit previous selections (if needed)**
   - Click "Edit" next to any section
   - Or use the back button to return to a previous step
   - Make your changes
   - Return to confirmation

3. **On mobile devices**
   - Click "View Selections" button to open confirmation drawer
   - Review all details in the sliding panel
   - Close drawer to return to current step

---

### 9. Enter Patient Information / Verify Identity

**Purpose:** Provide or verify your contact information.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **For new patients:**
   - Enter your first name
   - Enter your last name
   - Enter your phone number
   - Enter your email address (if required)
   - Enter your date of birth

2. **For returning patients:**
   - Enter your phone number
   - Verify via SMS code (MFA)
   - System retrieves your existing information

3. **Complete MFA verification (if required)**
   - Enter the verification code sent to your phone
   - Code is valid for a limited time
   - Request new code if expired

---

### 10. Select Payment Method

**Purpose:** Choose how you'll pay for the appointment (if pre-payment required).

**When This Appears:**
- Practice requires deposit or full payment upfront
- Service requires pre-payment

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **View payment options**
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Apple Pay / Google Pay (if available)
   - Pay at Visit (if allowed)

2. **Select payment method**
   - Click on your preferred option
   - Card input fields appear if paying by card

3. **Enter card details (if applicable)**
   - Card number
   - Expiration date (MM/YY)
   - CVV/Security code
   - Billing ZIP code

4. **Proceed to checkout**
   - Click "Continue" or "Pay Now"

---

### 11. Complete Checkout and Payment

**Purpose:** Finalize the booking and process payment.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Review final summary**
   - All appointment details
   - Total amount due
   - Cancellation policy

2. **Accept terms and conditions**
   - Read the terms and cancellation policy
   - Check the acceptance checkbox

3. **Complete payment (if required)**
   - Click "Book Appointment" or "Pay & Book"
   - Wait for payment processing

4. **View booking confirmation**
   - Confirmation number displayed
   - Appointment details confirmed
   - Calendar invite option may appear

---

### 12. View Booking Completion

**Purpose:** Receive confirmation of successful booking.

**What You'll See:**

1. **Success message**
   - "Appointment Booked!" or similar confirmation
   - Green checkmark or success icon

2. **Booking details**
   - Date and time
   - Provider name
   - Location/address
   - Confirmation number

3. **Next steps**
   - Add to calendar options
   - Reminders about forms to complete
   - Contact information for questions

4. **Actions available:**
   - "Add to Calendar" - Downloads .ics file
   - "View Appointment" - See full details
   - "Book Another" - Start new booking

---

### 13. Reschedule or Cancel Existing Appointment

**Purpose:** Modify or cancel a previously booked appointment.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Click "Reschedule or Cancel" button**
   - Located at the bottom of the first booking step
   - Button is styled as a floating pill

2. **Complete MFA verification**
   - MFA dialog opens
   - Enter your phone number
   - Enter the verification code

3. **View existing appointment**
   - Your upcoming appointment is displayed
   - Shows date, time, service, and provider

4. **Choose your action:**

   **To Reschedule:**
   - Click "Reschedule" button
   - Select new date and time
   - Confirm the change

   **To Cancel:**
   - Click "Cancel Appointment" button
   - Confirm cancellation in the dialog
   - Cancellation is processed

5. **Receive confirmation**
   - Success message confirms your action
   - Email/SMS confirmation sent

**Restrictions:**
- Cancellation may be restricted within certain time of appointment
- Practice policies may apply

---

### 14. Call the Clinic for Help

**Purpose:** Get phone support for booking questions.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Locate the phone number**
   - Displayed in the footer of the page
   - Format: "Need help? Call us at [phone number]"

2. **Click the phone number**
   - On mobile: Opens phone dialer
   - On desktop: May open communication app

3. **Or manually dial**
   - Note the number
   - Call from any phone

---

### 15. Navigate Back to Previous Steps

**Purpose:** Return to earlier steps to modify selections.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Locate the back button**
   - Arrow icon in the top-left area
   - Or "Back" text link

2. **Click the back button**
   - Returns to the previous step
   - Previous selections are preserved

3. **Continue navigation**
   - Click back multiple times to reach earlier steps
   - Your progress is maintained

---

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓

        ↓

## Mobile-Specific Features

- **Confirmation drawer:** Swipe-up panel to view selections
- **Touch-optimized calendar:** Easy date selection
- **Click-to-call:** Tap phone numbers to call
- **Responsive layout:** Full functionality on all screen sizes

## Accessibility Features

- Full keyboard navigation
- Screen reader announcements for step changes
- Clear focus indicators
- High contrast mode support
- Time slots include full date/time in accessible format

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| No available times | Try different dates or providers |
| Payment fails | Check card details, try different card |
| Page stuck loading | Refresh and try again |
| Verification code not received | Request new code, check SMS spam |
| Progress lost | Browser may have cleared storage, restart booking |
| Provider not showing | Provider may not offer selected service |
