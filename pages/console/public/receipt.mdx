# Public Receipt

The receipt page displays payment confirmation details to patients after a successful transaction.

## Receipt Contents

- Practice name and contact
- Patient name
- Payment date and time
- Amount paid
- Payment method (last 4 digits)
- Transaction ID
- Items paid for
