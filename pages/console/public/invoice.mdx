# Public Invoice

The public invoice page displays a detailed invoice to patients with the option to pay online.

## View Invoice

Patients see:
- Practice information
- Patient details
- Line items with descriptions
- Subtotal, taxes, and total
- Payment history
- Outstanding balance

## Pay Invoice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review charges">
    Verify invoice details
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Pay Now">
    Opens payment form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment details">
    Card information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Payment applied to invoice
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Download PDF

Click **Download** to save a PDF copy of the invoice.
