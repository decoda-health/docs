# Pay Now

The Pay Now page allows patients to view and pay their outstanding balances online.

<Info>
This is a public page. Patients access via links sent in payment reminders.
</Info>

## Make Payment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access payment page">
    Click link from SMS, email, or invoice
  </Step>
  <Step title="View balance">
    See outstanding charges and total due
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter payment details">
    Credit/debit card information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit payment">
    Payment processed securely
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive confirmation">
    Receipt displayed and emailed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Payment Options

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pay Full Balance" icon="money-bill">
    Pay entire outstanding amount
  </Card>
  <Card title="Partial Payment" icon="money-bill-transfer">
    Pay a portion of balance
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Accepted Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards
- HSA/FSA cards

## Security

- PCI-compliant payment processing
- SSL encrypted connection
- No card data stored on our servers
