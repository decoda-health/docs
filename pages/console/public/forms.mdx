# Public Forms

The public forms page allows patients to complete intake and consent forms. Forms are sent via link and can be completed on any device.

<Info>
This is a public page. Patients access via unique links sent by SMS or email.
</Info>

## Complete Form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open form link">
    Patient clicks link from message
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out sections">
    Complete all required fields
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign if required">
    Provide electronic signature
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Form saved and staff notified
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form Features

- Progress indicator shows completion
- Save and continue later
- Upload attachments if enabled
- Signature capture
- Multiple language support

## After Submission

- Confirmation displayed
- Copy sent to patient email (if configured)
- Staff receives notification
- Submission appears in patient record
