# Contact Us

The Contact Us page provides a public form for potential and existing patients to reach the practice.

## Submit Inquiry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fill contact form">
    Name, email, phone, message
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select inquiry type">
    General, appointment, billing, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Inquiry sent to practice
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Form Fields

| Field | Required |
|-------|----------|
| Name | Yes |
| Email | Yes |
| Phone | Optional |
| Message | Yes |
| Inquiry Type | Optional |

## After Submission

- Confirmation displayed
- Email sent to practice staff
- Auto-response to submitter (if configured)
