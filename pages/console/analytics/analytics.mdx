# Analytics & Reports

Analytics helps you understand how your practice is performing. Create custom dashboards to track revenue, appointments, patient trends, and more - all in visual charts and reports you can customize.

## What Can You Track?

<CardGroup cols={2}>

  </Card>
  <Card title="Appointments" icon="calendar-check">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Patient Trends" icon="users">

## Viewing Your Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Analytics">
    Click **Analytics** in the main menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="See your widgets">
    Each chart or number on the dashboard is called a "widget"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust the date range">
    Use the date selector at the top to change what time period you're looking at
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Changing the date range updates all widgets at once, so you can quickly compare different time periods.
</Tip>

## Types of Widgets

### Numbers
Big, bold numbers showing key metrics like total revenue or patient count.

### Progress Circles
Show how close you are to a goal - like monthly revenue targets.

### Charts
Visual representations of your data:
- **Area charts** - Great for showing trends over time
- **Bar charts** - Perfect for comparing categories (like revenue by service)
- **Donut charts** - Show how things break down (like payment methods used)

### Tables
Detailed data in rows and columns, sortable by clicking headers.

## Creating a New Widget

Want to track something specific? Create your own widget:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Create Widget">
    Usually in the top right corner of the dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Give it a name">
    Something descriptive like "Weekly Revenue by Provider"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose what to measure">
    Pick your data source (appointments, payments, patients, etc.)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure how to display it">
    - What to count or sum
    - How to group the data
    - Any filters to apply
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a chart type">
    You'll see a preview as you go
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Your widget appears on the dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Editing Widgets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the widget">
    Locate it on your dashboard
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click the gear icon">
    Or click the menu (three dots) and select Edit
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your changes">
    Adjust the title, data, or visualization
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Changes apply immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Rearranging Your Dashboard

Want widgets in a different order?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Edit Layout">
    Enables drag-and-drop mode
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag widgets around">
    Click and hold a widget, then drag it where you want
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Layout">
    Locks in your arrangement
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Diving Deeper

Click on any part of a chart (a bar, a slice, a data point) to see more details. For example:
- Click a bar in a "Revenue by Service" chart to see which specific transactions made up that total
- Click a slice in a donut chart to filter and see just those records

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who can see the analytics dashboard?">
    This depends on your role and permissions. Typically, managers and administrators have full access. Ask your admin if you need access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How often does the data update?">
    Data updates in real-time. As new appointments, payments, or other activities happen, the dashboard reflects them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I share a widget with someone?">
    You can export widgets as images or PDFs to share. The dashboard itself is shared across your team based on permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why does my widget show no data?">
    Check your date range and filters. You might be looking at a time period with no matching records.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Widget not loading | Refresh the page, check your internet connection |
| Data seems wrong | Double-check the date range at the top |
| Can't create widgets | You may not have permission - check with your admin |
| Export not downloading | Check if your browser is blocking popups |
