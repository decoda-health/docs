# Gallery Page - User Actions Guide

## Overview

The Gallery page displays all medical attachments and images across patients. Staff can browse, filter by patient and date range, and view medical documentation. Useful for quickly finding photos, documents, and scans associated with patient visits.

---

## Available Actions

### 1. View All Attachments

**Purpose:** Browse all medical attachments.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Gallery**
   - Scribe menu → Gallery

2. **View attachment grid**
   - Thumbnails displayed
   - All patients shown by default

---

### 2. Filter by Patient

**Purpose:** See attachments for specific patient.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Locate "Patient" filter**
   - Top right (desktop)
   - Settings popover (mobile)

2. **Type patient name**
   - Search dropdown appears

3. **Select patient from results**

4. **Gallery updates**
   - Shows only that patient's attachments
   - Upload button appears when patient selected

---

### 3. Filter by Date Range

**Purpose:** Find attachments from specific period.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Locate "Date Range" filter**
   - Top right (desktop)
   - Settings popover (mobile)

2. **Click date picker**

3. **Select range:**
   - Use preset (Today, This Week, etc.)
   - Or pick custom dates

4. **Gallery updates**

---

### 4. Clear Filters

**Purpose:** Show all attachments again.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Clear patient filter**
   - Click X on patient selector

2. **Clear date range**
   - Click X on date picker

---

### 5. Upload Attachment (Patient Selected)

**Purpose:** Add new attachment for patient.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Select a patient first**
   - Upload only available with patient

2. **Click upload button**
   - In gallery section

3. **Select file**

4. **File uploads**

---

### 6. View Attachment Details

**Purpose:** See full-size image or document.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Click on attachment thumbnail**

2. **Full viewer opens**
   - Zoom controls available
   - Navigation if multiple

---

### 7. Mobile Filter Access

**Purpose:** Access filters on small screens.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Click settings icon**
   - Top left header area

2. **Popover opens**
   - Patient filter
   - Date range filter

3. **Apply filters**

4. **Click outside to close**

## Date Range Presets

Available quick selections:
- Today
- Yesterday
- Last 7 Days
- Last 30 Days
- This Month
- Last Month
- Custom Range

---

## Gallery Modes

| Mode | Trigger | Features |
|------|---------|----------|
| Global | No patient selected | View all attachments |
| Patient | Patient selected | View patient's attachments, can upload |
