# Smart Suggestions

Configure AI-powered suggestions for improved efficiency.

## Suggestion Types

- **Service suggestions**: Recommend services based on history
- **Product suggestions**: Upsell recommendations
- **Note suggestions**: Common phrases and templates
- **Time suggestions**: Optimal appointment times

## Enable/Disable

Toggle individual suggestion types based on your workflow preferences.

## Learning

The AI learns from:
- Historical patterns
- Provider preferences
- Patient behavior
- Seasonal trends
