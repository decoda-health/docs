# Payment Mediums

Configure which payment types are accepted at your practice.

## Available Mediums

| Medium | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Card** | Credit and debit cards |
| **Cash** | Physical cash payments |
| **Check** | Paper checks |
| **POS Terminal** | In-person card readers |
| **Patient Credit** | Applied credit balance |
| **Insurance** | Insurance payments |
