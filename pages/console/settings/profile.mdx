# Profile Settings

Manage your personal account information and preferences.

## Personal Information

- First name, last name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile photo

## Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Change Password">
    Opens password form
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter current password">
    Verify identity
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter new password">
    Must meet requirements
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Password updated
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Two-Factor Authentication

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable 2FA">
    Click to set up
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR code">
    With authenticator app
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter code">
    Verify setup
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Notification Preferences

Configure which notifications you receive via email or push.
