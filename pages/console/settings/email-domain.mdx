# Email Domain

Configure custom email domain for sending practice emails.

## Custom Domain Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add domain">
    Enter your domain name
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add DNS records">
    Configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Confirm domain ownership
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits

- Emails from your domain (e.g., noreply@yourpractice.com)
- Improved deliverability
- Professional appearance
- Consistent branding

## DNS Records Required

| Record | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| **SPF** | Authorize sending |
| **DKIM** | Sign emails |
| **DMARC** | Delivery policy |
