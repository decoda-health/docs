# Inbound Texting Assistant

Configure the AI assistant that responds to incoming text messages from patients.

## Configure Texting

- **Response style**: Formal or casual
- **Automated responses**: Common questions
- **Handoff rules**: When to escalate to human
- **Available hours**: When AI responds

## Capabilities

The AI can handle:
- Appointment inquiries
- Office hours questions
- Directions and location
- General FAQs
- Appointment confirmations

## Escalation

Configure when to notify staff:
- Urgent keywords
- Sentiment detection
- Unanswered questions
- After-hours messages
