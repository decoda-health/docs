# Payment Settings

Configure payment processing, accepted methods, and billing preferences.

## Payment Processing

- **Processor**: Rainforest Payments configuration
- **Merchant Account**: Account details
- **Processing Fees**: Fee structure

## Accepted Methods

Toggle payment methods:
- Credit/Debit cards
- Cash
- Check
- HSA/FSA
- Insurance

## Payment Plans

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable payment plans">
    Allow installment payments
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure options">
    Min/max installments, frequency
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set requirements">
    Minimum down payment
  </Step>
</Steps>

Manage connected point-of-sale devices for in-person payments.
