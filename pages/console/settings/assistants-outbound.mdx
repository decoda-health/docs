# Outbound AI Assistant

Configure the AI assistant for making outbound calls like appointment reminders and confirmations.

## Configure Outbound

- **Call purpose**: Reminders, confirmations, follow-ups
- **Voice selection**: Choose voice style
- **Script templates**: Customize messages
- **Timing**: When to make calls
