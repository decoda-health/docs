# Scheduling Settings

Configure how appointments are booked, cancelled, and managed.

## Booking Rules

- **Advance Booking**: How far ahead patients can book
- **Minimum Notice**: Required lead time for bookings
- **Cancellation Policy**: Required notice for cancellations
- **No-Show Fees**: Automatic fee for missed appointments

## Time Slots

- **Slot Duration**: Default appointment length
- **Buffer Time**: Gap between appointments
- **Business Hours**: When bookings are allowed
