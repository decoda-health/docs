# Calendar Appearance

Customize how the appointment calendar looks and behaves.

## Display Options

- **Default View**: Day, week, or month
- **Week Start**: Sunday or Monday
- **Time Format**: 12-hour or 24-hour
- **Show Weekends**: Include Sat/Sun

## Color Coding

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provider colors">
    Assign unique colors to providers
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status colors">
    Colors for different appointment states
  </Step>
  <Step title="Service colors">
    Optional colors by service type
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Time Slots

- **Slot height**: Pixel height per time slot
- **Time increments**: 15, 30, or 60 minutes
- **Working hours display**: Highlight business hours
