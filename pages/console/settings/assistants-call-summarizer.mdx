# Call Summarizer

Configure how AI generates summaries of phone calls.

## Summary Settings

- **Auto-summarize**: Generate for all calls
- **Summary length**: Brief or detailed
- **Key points extraction**: Important action items
- **Sentiment analysis**: Call tone detection

## Summary Sections

- Call purpose
- Patient questions
- Staff responses
- Action items
- Follow-up needed
