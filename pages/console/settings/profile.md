# Profile Page - User Actions Guide

## Overview

The Profile page allows users to manage their personal information including profile picture, contact details, and bio. Changes auto-save as you type.

---

## Available Actions

### 1. Update Profile Picture

**Purpose:** Change your display photo.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Profile**
   - Settings → Profile

2. **Click on profile picture**
   - Or upload area

3. **Select image file**
   - JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - Max 5MB

4. **Image uploads**
   - Shows immediately

---

### 2. Update First Name

**Purpose:** Change your first name.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Find Personal Information section**

2. **Edit First Name field**

3. **Auto-saves after pause**
   - Save indicator shows

---

### 3. Update Last Name

**Purpose:** Change your last name.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Edit Last Name field**

2. **Auto-saves after pause**

---

### 4. Update Email

**Purpose:** Change your email address.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Edit Email field**

2. **Must be valid format**
   - Validation shown

3. **Auto-saves**

---

### 5. Update Phone Number

**Purpose:** Change your phone number.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Edit Phone Number field**

2. **Auto-formats as you type**

3. **Auto-saves**

---

### 6. Update Bio

**Purpose:** Add/edit your biography.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Find About You section**

2. **Edit bio text area**
   - Shown on self-scheduling page

3. **Auto-saves**

---

## Sections

| Section | Fields |
|---------|--------|
| Profile Picture | Photo upload |
| Personal Information | Name, email, phone |
| About You | Bio text |

---

## Auto-Save

- Changes save automatically
- 1 second debounce
- Save status indicator:
  - Saving...
  - Saved
  - Error

---

## Profile Picture Requirements

| Requirement | Value |
|-------------|-------|
| Formats | JPG, PNG, WebP |
| Max size | 5MB |
| Display | Circular crop |

---

## Bio Usage

Your bio appears:
- On self-scheduling page
- Provider selection for patients
- About the provider

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
