# Scheduling Page - User Actions Guide

## Overview

The Scheduling page configures appointment booking settings including self-scheduling options, capacity limits, and blacklisted time slots. Controls how patients can book and what limits are enforced.

---

## Available Actions

### 1. Configure Self-Scheduling

**Purpose:** Control patient booking options.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Navigate to Scheduling**
   - Settings → Scheduling

2. **View Self-Scheduling section**

3. **Configure options:**
   - Enable/disable self-scheduling
   - Booking window settings
   - Cancellation policies

---

### 2. Set Capacity Limits

**Purpose:** Limit simultaneous appointments.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Find Capacity section**

2. **Configure limits:**
   - Per provider
   - Per location
   - Per time slot

3. **Save changes**

---

### 3. Manage Blacklist

**Purpose:** Block specific time slots.

**Step-by-Step Instructions:**

1. **Find Blacklist section**

2. **Add blocked times:**
   - Select date/time range
   - Recurring or one-time

3. **View existing blocks**

4. **Remove blocks as needed**

---

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Patients can't book | Check self-scheduling enabled |
| Overbooking | Review capacity limits |
